Files for attaching potentiometers to the OWI arm.  
* 3D prints are the 5 parts that must be 3D printed:
  * M1Holder
  * M3Tip
  * M5Holder 
  * and 2x of M34Holder
* LaserCut are the pieces that can be laser cut from 6mm and from 2mm acrylic.
* LaserCutPartsConvertedTo3Dprints has stl and solid works versions of the laser cut parts suitable for 3D printing
* multiplePartsForClass are files that economically arrange parts for printing many at a time.
